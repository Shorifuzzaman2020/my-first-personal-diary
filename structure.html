About my village

Khushalpur, a charming village in Sreebardi Thana, Sherpur district, Mymensingh division, is a haven of natural beauty and tranquility. With lush green fields, serene ponds, and a strong sense of community, the village showcases the simplicity of rural life. The fertile land supports thriving agriculture, and the villagers’ warm hospitality reflects their rich cultural heritage. Whether it’s the vibrant festivals or the breathtaking sunsets, Khushalpur offers an unforgettable experience, reminding visitors of the timeless joys of living close to nature.

About my school life

Alhaz Fazlul Haque Memorial Biddaniketon, located in Sreebardi Thana, Sherpur district, holds a special place in my heart. This school was not just a place for learning but a stage where countless memories of my childhood and adolescence were created. From class 1 to class 10, I was surrounded by an amazing circle of friends who made every moment unforgettable. The school’s environment was vibrant, filled with the cheerful chatter of students and the guidance of dedicated teachers. Together, we experienced the joys of learning, the thrill of sports, and the excitement of annual cultural programs. My friends and I shared countless moments, from playing games in the playground to studying for exams, each memory adding a unique color to the canvas of my school life. Even today, the memories of Alhaz Fazlul Haque Memorial Biddaniketon bring a smile to my face. It was a time of growth, laughter, and lifelong friendships that I will always cherish.

About my college life

Agricultural University College, nestled within the serene campus of Bangladesh Agricultural University in the Mymensingh division, is a place I hold dear. Its natural beauty, with lush greenery and a tranquil atmosphere, provided the perfect backdrop for two years of memorable college life. Unlike school, my college friendships were fewer but just as meaningful. In these two years, I built strong bonds with a few friends who made this short journey unforgettable. The guidance and support of my college teachers were truly remarkable, fostering both personal and academic growth. The environment of the college, enriched with knowledge and harmony, inspired me every day. Whether walking through the verdant paths or attending classes in the warm, welcoming atmosphere, every moment spent here added to my love for the institution. Agricultural University College will always remain a cherished chapter in my life, reminding me of the beauty of short but impactful experiences.
